We've added controller support here at TazUO for all your steam decks, legion gos and rog allies starting in 3.15.0!  

# Information
`Left joystick` will move your character / Button down for right click  
`Right joystick` will move your mouse / Button down for left click  
`Menu button` open and close the options menu  

Most other buttons can be bound to Macros the same way you would bind keyboard keys in the menu.  

# Settings
`Options->TazUO->Controller` or type `-optlink 15:1023`
